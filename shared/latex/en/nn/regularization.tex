\begin{frame}{Weight penalty}
  Penalize neurons with big weights with a term in the loss function:

  \[
  \text{Loss} = \text{Loss} + \lambda\sum||w||^2
  \]

  Where $\lambda$ is a hyperparameter.
\end{frame}
